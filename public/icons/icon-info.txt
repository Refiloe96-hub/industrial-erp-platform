PWA Icon Placeholders

For production deployment, replace these placeholders with actual branded icons.

Required icon sizes:
- 72x72
- 96x96
- 128x128
- 144x144
- 152x152
- 192x192 (recommended for home screen)
- 384x384
- 512x512 (recommended for splash screen)

Icon should:
- Be simple and recognizable
- Work on light and dark backgrounds
- Represent industrial/manufacturing theme
- Be visible at small sizes

Use tools like:
- Figma / Adobe Illustrator
- PWA Asset Generator
- RealFaviconGenerator

Color scheme suggestion:
- Primary: Industrial blue (#2563eb)
- Secondary: Manufacturing gray (#1a1a1a)
- Accent: Success green (#10b981)
